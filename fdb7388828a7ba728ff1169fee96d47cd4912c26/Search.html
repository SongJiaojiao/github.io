Background

Search is the gateway to Marquee's vast data such as research, assets, analytics, etc. Historically, the search was built on legacy systems and owned by an external engine. As Marquee expands its digital products, managing this system became increasinly challenging..

In 2023, we started revamping the search experience from ground up.

Problems

I quickly delved into the data, analyzed more than 50,000 search queries, and interviewed over 20 of our most frequent users.

"A bag of words"

Typical search methods look for exact word matches, but this can cause problems when some words get more attention than others. This imbalance can lead to poor search results that don't match what users are looking for.

A need to understand users' underlying search intents

Based on the research,people tend to search for four main things:company, asset, author, and region. However, the existing search mechanism falls short in addressing these specific needs, calling for a more tailored search approach.
