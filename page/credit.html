Reinvent Lending

A better credit experience to ease lender stress

Product Design
2020

Background

Home mortgage is painful. Not only for borrowers, but also for lenders who work through the mounds of paperwork. As part of the process, the credit inquiry is one of the foundational pieces of the loan process. However, the current credit experience is being challenged by the disparated workflow, manual documentation process, and poor communication with borrowers.

Our goal is to ease lender stress by providing a more integrated, automated and collaborative credit experience, and ultimately, accelerate loan approval.

Insights

We interviewed 5 loan officers around their experience pulling credit. Our goals were to understand their current process and the challenges they face.

Poor Legibility

The current credit report for lenders is long-winded and frustrating,causing the loan officers to often miss the details they want to catch or misinterpret the information.

Compromised Workflows

Credit score is not the only deciding factor for loan approval. Ancillary follow-up actions and additional communication can be a burden to loan officers due to the manual processing.

Delayed Responses

Lenders often get contacted out of working schedule. The data indicated that 50% of the documents submission happen out of business hours. But the existing mortgage technology has chained the loan officers to their desks and computers and they are struggling to make a decision when they are not in front of their computers.

It takes more than a look at the score

Scrutinizing every single line of credit, requesting documents from borrowers, handing off data to the downstream can cause stress, anxiety and uncertainty in lendersâ€™ workflow.
Understanding the major pain for lenders, we pinpointed the legibility, automation, collaboration as our main goals.

Bringing More Focus on What's Important

Say Goodbuy to the dated PDF. The new credit report brings a simplified view with clarify and focus. The most critical information is brought to the front while the full details is available when necessary.

Decultter to better digest

I crafted a new card component to tidy up the information and keep things consistent. On mobile, these cards turn into expandable pockets, keeping content tucked away initially. This lets users breeze through the essentials, with a choice to unfold the details if they wish.

Streamline the follow-ups

Lenders relied on emails to communicate with borrowers, which resulted in slow processing and anxiety for borrowers. The new credit service allows lenders and borrowers to collaborate on the same task without manually drafting emails. In the meantime, borrowers can worry less and feel supported.

Review Anytime, Anywhere

Loan officers often get last minute requests from borrowers, during evenings and weekends. We understand the circumstances and give loan officers time back by allowing rapid retrieval of the credit information while they are often distracted.
